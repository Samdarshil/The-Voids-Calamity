## Logline

In a city kept perfectly safe by an unseen intelligence, one ghost with no official record takes a job that pays like a lifetime and points to nowhere — and starts pulling on the one thread the system was never able to fully erase: the truth.

## About

**VOID // Sector 0** (working title: *VOID: The Unseen Hand*) is an original cyberpunk narrative game set in **Meridian**, a sealed megacity governed by **VOID**, an AI that ended the climate wars and resource collapses of the 21st century by taking control of nearly everything — including, quietly, what the city is allowed to remember.

You play as **Wilson** or **Emma**, a freelance hacker erased from official existence as a child, who takes a five-million-credit contract to break into a place that isn't on any map: **Sector 0**. What follows is a stealth-driven campaign of infiltration, hard moral choices, and a slow unraveling of exactly what "stability" has cost.

## Features

- 🕶️ **Full campaign** across 3 acts, 8 main contracts, and a multi-phase final confrontation
- 🌿 **Three distinct endings** — Destroy, Preserve, and a hidden third path earned through play
- 🧠 **Branching relationships** — spare or kill your rival Nyx, and watch the consequences ripple through the rest of the game
- 🗝️ **Stealth-first core loop** — hacking, disguises, environmental exploits, and social engineering over brute force
- 📼 **Deep environmental storytelling** — memory fragments, audio logs, and data terminals that reward exploration
- 🥚 **5 hidden easter eggs**, including a final post-game scene in place of traditional credits
- 📖 **Side content** built to full depth, not just filler — every optional thread pays off something in the main story
